MED: User Study (GD 2019)

  • Quantitative Results:
    • response time similar to CED
    • indications for best accuracy
  • Qualitative Results (User Responses):
    • Morphing improves readability
    • "It is messy and difficult to see. My eyes are strained."
    • "The stubs change too fast. The time for stubs to connect is too short."
  • Consequences in GD2022 Paper:
    • much slower morphing speed (in GD2019 inspired by human limits to minimize time)
    • some delay at full edge length
